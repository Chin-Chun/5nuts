The Problem

Australia has one of the highest rates of food allergies in the world. Accordingly, nuts and dairy allergies top the chart with increase in cases amongst your children living in Victoria.


The Solution

Five nuts focuses on popularising the knowledge of food allergies, along with related medical knowledge and first aid methods, to help Victorian residents better understand the potential health risks of food allergies and raise the awareness for prevention.
