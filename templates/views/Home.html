Five nuts focuses on popularising the knowledge of food allergies, along with related medical knowledge and first aid methods, to help Victorian residents better understand the potential health risks of food allergies and raise the awareness for prevention.


What is Food Allergy?

An adverse reaction by the immune system towards a certain type of food is termed as Food Allergy. For many sensitive children, minute allergic reaction can be frightening and life-threatening in extreme cases which leads to immediate hospitalization.

Overview

Australia has one of the highest rates of food allergies in the world. Accordingly nuts and dairy allergies top the chart with increase in cases amongst youn children living in Victoria.
