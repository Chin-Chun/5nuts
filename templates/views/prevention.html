For pregnant

  1. Introduce the concept of a balanced diet which includes all the vitamins and minerals need for the mother and the baby.
  2. Take extra care of the mental health too. Research suggests that the state of the brain and the heart affects the child.
  3. Do not exclude any foods which contains high minerals and vitamins.
  4. Include omega intake which include foods like canola oil, soybean oil, salmon, seafood items.
